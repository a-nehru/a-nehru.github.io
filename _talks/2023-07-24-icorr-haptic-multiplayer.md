Presented a pilot study evaluating haptic multiplayer training paradigms using the PLUTO hand rehabilitation robot. Three game modes were tested — solo, collaborative, and competitive — in 12 unimpaired participants (6 dyads). Collaborative mode was preferred by 10/12 participants, and both social modes improved solo performance metrics compared to solo training alone.
