Served as lead student organizer and teaching assistant for the [NeuroRehack Summer School](https://www.neurorehack.com) in 2021 and 2024 — a virtual program that attracted over 120 participants from around the world.

The program focused on neurorehabilitation and consisted of two weeks of lectures followed by four weeks of hands-on project training. Responsibilities included:

- Planning and organizing the full curriculum and course materials
- Managing communications with international participants
- Providing technical guidance and mentorship during hands-on project sessions
- Received the **Best TA Award (2021)** for contributions to the program
