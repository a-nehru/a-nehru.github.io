## Current Position

**2024–Present** — Postdoctoral Researcher, Northwestern University & Shirley Ryan AbilityLab, Chicago, USA  
*Supervisors: Dr. Milap Sandhu and Dr. W. Zev Rymer*

- **Principal Investigator, PVA Research Fellow (FY2026):** Awarded a Paralyzed Veterans of America Research Foundation grant to lead a funded RCT investigating Myoelectric Interface Neurorehabilitation Training (MINT) combined with Acute Intermittent Hypoxia (AIH) to enhance motor recovery in MS.
- **NIH R21 randomized crossover trial (MS):** Led a feasibility and safety study evaluating five consecutive days of AIH in individuals with MS (n=27). Trial completed; manuscripts in preparation.
- **Mechanistic AIH studies:** Investigating AIH-related effects on proprioception and fatigue in individuals with and without MS.
- **VR-based motor assessment:** Developed a Meta Quest 3 system to quantify upper-limb motor impairment post-stroke.
- **Proprioception assessment framework:** Designed and validated a hierarchical robotic wrist proprioception battery (JDT, J-to-J, J-to-V) with psychometric benchmarks for clinical trials.
- **Inpatient rehabilitation gaming system:** Developing a game-based system to increase upper-limb training dosage during inpatient stroke rehabilitation.

**2025–Present** — Scientific and Technical Advisor, Thryv Mobility Rehabilitation Solutions Pvt. Ltd., India

## Research Projects

**PLUTO — Plug-and-Train Robot for Hand Rehabilitation** *(PhD Thesis, 2018–2023)*  
Modular single-DOF hand rehabilitation robot for training five isolated hand movements. Evaluated through three in-clinic studies and a four-week home trial (first of its kind in India). Deployed across six centers; 1,000+ patients. Commercially available since May 2025.

**MARS — Motorized Arm Support** *(2022–2024, Patent Pending)*  
Compact underactuated arm support providing adaptive de-weighting for clinic or home rehabilitation.

**AREBO — Arm Rehabilitation Robot** *(2022–2023)*  
Self-aligning end-effector robot for single- and multi-joint upper-limb rehabilitation.

**HyperCube** *(2021–2022, Patent Pending)*  
Portable multi-surface device with integrated sensors for hand rehabilitation and assessment.

**Vegas — Vestibular Gaming System** *(Master's Thesis, 2018–2019)*  
Smartphone-based HMD system for vestibular rehabilitation. High usability (SUS > 80), minimal simulator sickness in 30 participants.
