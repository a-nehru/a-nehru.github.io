**Clinical Trial:** [NCT06390930](https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT06390930)

---

Presented the within-day test-retest reliability of a three-task robotic wrist proprioception battery, spanning single-frame and cross-frame judgments.

**Tasks (using the PLUTO wrist robot):**
- **JDT** — joint detection threshold (passive movement, single frame) — ICC = 0.959
- **J-to-J** — joint-to-joint angle matching (same frame) — ICC = 0.837
- **J-to-V** — joint-to-visual matching, requiring cross-frame coordinate transformation — ICC = 0.769

Error scaled monotonically with task complexity (JDT < J-to-J < J-to-V), consistent with the low-to-high proprioceptive hierarchy. SDC₉₅ values (0.85°, 1.71°, 3.54°) provide thresholds for detecting true change in future clinical trials.
